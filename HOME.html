Facilities

Rbs supports an education system that lays emphasis on practice-oriented learning. The focus is on motivating students to explore new ideas and learn by doing. The school has a unique Model Workshop, in addition to the labs and library, where the students can prepare their exhibits, under the guidance of an expert for Intra School and Inter School presentations.

Library

The school's two-floor library is imaginatively designed to stock huge collection of books that cover fiction and non-fiction in all genres and subjects. A huge section of the ground floor is allocated for the Pre-Primary, where children participate in interactive story sessions.

Digital Media Wing

This wing has a good collection of educational, digital and multimedia materials (CDs, DVDs and videos) for use in the classes and with an Internet corner.

Labs

Well-equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Life Sciences and Mathematics, provide the students ample opportunities to question, explore, examine and hypothesize.
